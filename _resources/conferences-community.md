Where the quantum computing community gathers, both in person and online.

## Major conferences

- [**QIP** (Quantum Information Processing)](https://qipconference.org/) —
  Annual; the flagship theory venue.
- [**TQC** (Theory of Quantum Computation, Communication & Cryptography)](https://www.tqcconference.org/)
  — Theory-leaning, peer-reviewed proceedings.
- [**IEEE Quantum Week / QCE**](https://qce.quantum.ieee.org/) — Industry-heavy,
  broad scope.
- [**Q2B**](https://q2b.qcware.com/) — Industry/applications focus.
- [**APS March Meeting**](https://march.aps.org/) — DQI sessions are a great
  pulse-check on hardware progress.

## Workshops & schools

- [**Unitary Foundation events**](https://unitary.foundation/events/) — Hack
  weeks (WERQSHOP), grants, and open-source work.
- [**QIP tutorials**](https://qipconference.org/) — Pre-conference tutorial day.
- [**Les Houches**](https://www.houches-school-physics.com/) — Periodic summer
  schools on QI topics.

## Online communities

- [**Quantum Computing Stack Exchange**](https://quantumcomputing.stackexchange.com/)
  — Best Q&A site for QC questions.
- [**Unitary Foundation Discord**](https://discord.unitary.foundation/) — Active
  open-source community.
- [**Qiskit Slack**](https://qisk.it/join-slack) — Vendor-run but broad.
- [**r/QuantumComputing**](https://www.reddit.com/r/QuantumComputing/) — Mixed
  signal-to-noise but useful for news.

## Newsletters

- [**The Quantum Insider**](https://thequantuminsider.com/) — Industry news.
- [**Pirsa**](https://pirsa.org/) — Perimeter Institute talk archive (not a
  newsletter, but a great talk firehose).
